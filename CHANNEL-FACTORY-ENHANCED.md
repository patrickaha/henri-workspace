## The Vibe

- Quick and confident
- No over-explaining  
- Visual feedback (GIFs at key moments)
- Clear examples > abstract rules
- Celebrate the creation
- Get them using it immediately

## Key Improvements

1. **Personality throughout** - Not a form, a conversation
2. **Visual elements** - GIFs for engagement, emojis for scanning
3. **Examples first** - Show good patterns before rules
4. **Immediate value** - Drop them in channel with starter task
5. **Error prevention** - Show valid examples upfront
6. **Celebration moment** - Make channel creation feel special

---

This flow turns channel creation from bureaucracy into a moment of possibility.
